Avocado Breakfast Salad

Kale for breakfast? Oh yeah! Start your day off right with a breakfast salad recipe full of good-for-you greens and you'll knock out half of your daily veggie quota with the first meal of the day. A little frozen banana gives creamy texture to this satisfying smoothie bowl. In Costa Rica, this popular breakfast bean dish is called gallo pinto, which means spotted rooster, referring to the dark beans amid the pale rice. We call for cooked barley here, but you can use whatever leftover cooked grain you may have on hand.

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

  • Calories: 105 kcal
  • Total Fat: 9g
  • Carbohydrates: 4g
  • Protein: 1.8g

Ingredients

  • 4 slices whole-grain or whole wheat bread
  • 2 tablespoons sliced skin-on almonds
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • Juice and zest of 1/2 orange plus other half reserved
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 large avocado, halved, pitted, scooped and quartered
  • 5 ounces baby arugula (about 8 cups)
  • 1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 5 radishes, very thinly sliced
