Chicken Quesadillas

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers and onions, coarsely chopped
  • 4 ounces shredded cooked chicken

  • 1/2 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
