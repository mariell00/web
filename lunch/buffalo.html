Buffalo Chicken

Get the flavors of spicy Buffalo chicken wings, without frying, in this nutritious couscous bowl loaded with protein and crisp veggies.

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

  • Calories: 579 kcal
  • Total Fat: 32g
  • Carbohydrates: 42g
  • Protein: 32g
